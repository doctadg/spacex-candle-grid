The mechanic is dead simple.

Fast enough for the IPO open, clear enough for casual players, strategic enough for chart people.

① Segment

Next candle becomes a grid

Each row is a price close band around the current market. Each column is a risk lane with a different multiplier.

② Lock

Players pick before countdown

Squares lock near the end of the candle. Late entries are blocked to keep the game fair and readable.

③ Settle

Close price selects winners

When the candle closes, the winning band lights up. Correct players get points, streaks, and leaderboard movement.
